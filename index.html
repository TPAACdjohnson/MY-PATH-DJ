🎮 Darryl's Story

🎮 This is not just a game — it's a psychological and spiritual experiment in truth. You are stepping into the real, lived experience of a Black boy raised in chaos, silence, love, and fire. Every decision you make has consequences. Each choice shifts your mental health stats: 🧠 Resilience, 💔 Trauma, 😤 Rage, 🌪️ Dissociation, 🔥 Authenticity, ✝️ Purpose. These choices are not hypothetical — they are biographical. This game is about survival, reflection, and reclaiming control over your narrative. It's built to mirror real trauma, test moral decision-making, and track the consequences across time. By the end, your path will lead to one of three brutally honest outcomes: 🕊️ Healed Leader • 🌘 Functional Survivor • 🧨 Hardened Soul Are you ready to walk this path? Choose your chapter. Your decisions shape your legacy.
